About

We are a Rugby club located in Columbus, Indiana focused on fostering a sustainable Rugby community by providing a family through healthy competition. The Columbus Rugby community includes a Men's Division III team, high school Boys and Girls team, as well as supporting a youth program through Columbus Parks and Rec Department. The CRFC has been supporting and promoting the growth of Rugby in Southern Indiana since 1997. Our high school Boys team won State Championships in both 2005 and 2012 and the Girls team was State runners-up in 2011.
